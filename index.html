How to make pancakes in four steps:

Ingredients

Preparation

1

In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. In another bowl, whisk together the milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla extract. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Do not overmix; a few lumps are fine.

2

Preheat a non-stick griddle or a large frying pan over medium heat. Lightly grease the griddle with butter or oil. You can test if the griddle is hot enough by sprinkling a few drops of water on it; if they sizzle and evaporate, the griddle is ready.

3

Pour about 1/4 cup of batter onto the griddle for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form on the surface of the pancakes and the edges start to look set, about 2-3 minutes. Flip the pancakes and cook for another 2-3 minutes, or until golden brown and cooked through.

4

Remove the pancakes from the griddle and keep them warm in a low oven or under a clean kitchen towel. Repeat with the remaining batter, adding more butter or oil to the griddle as needed. Serve the pancakes warm with your favorite toppings, such as maple syrup, fresh berries, or powdered sugar.
